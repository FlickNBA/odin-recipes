Pesto Pasta

Description

This is the best meal ever. It takes 15 minutes to prepare, takes minimal amount of dishes to wash and it is D E L I C I O U S.

Ingredients

  • 100 g of pasta - I recommend Barilla Bavette
  • 40 g of pesto - either green or sundried tomatoes one
  • a lot of parmesan
  • black pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil some water with good amount of salt.
  2. Cook the pasta according to informations on packaging.
  3. Add pesto and freshly grated parmesan.
  4. If you like an extra layer of flavor add some black pepper on top.
